Provided by Nobiax with Dactilardesign collaboration.


- 5 complex plant meshes and textures: fern, palm, bamboo, tropical plant and 
palviflora.
- 1 grass mesh and 6 different textures.
- 3 simple plant meshes with 20 compatible textures.
- 5 detailed plants with flower.

Each asset may contain diffuse, bump, specular and/or other maps, 512 and 1024
squared pixel size.

______________________________

How to use:

- Select the desired prefab from "prefabs" folder and drag and drop in the scene,
or use with Unity terrain system.
- Check texture import details and make sure you use the texture size you need.
